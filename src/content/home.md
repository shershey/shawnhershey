# Shawn Hershey

Dancer, musician, and software developer based in New York City.

Shawn has been swing and blues dancing since 1999 and teaching since 2002. He's a trumpet player and bandleader of [The Fried Bananas](https://www.friedbananas.com), a swing and blues band based in New York.

He currently runs [Thursday Practice](https://nycbalboa.com/thursday-practice), a weekly Balboa social in Midtown Manhattan.
